Founder's Story

After moving to the city car nuts Molly & Tim quickly realized it’s not so easy to do regular maintenance without the space. In talking with fellow enthusiasts they decided a shared garage was needed to fulfill their urban lifestyle.

While researching the concept their idea grew and grew. It turned out that urban gearheads aren’t the only ones who need this space. Using lifts makes many jobs much easier and there are lots of people who just don’t know about the machine they drive every day but would like to learn.

Gearhead Workspace aims to help all of these people. From those who don’t want anyone else working on their car to those who don’t know where to start. Cars are amazing machines and we want to help you get the most enjoyment you can from yours.

Meet The Team

Tim Heyen – Founder

When Tim was six he told his parents he wanted a Corvette. By the age of fifteen he saved enough money to buy his first car: a fixer upper that he lovingly brought back to life on his own. He eventually did get the Vette he wanted and is now a member of two Corvette clubs and regularly attends high performance driving events.

Molly Heyen – CoFounder

If Molly has any regrets in life it’s losing the picture she took as a child of the Ferrari Testarossa at her first visit to the Chicago Auto Show. It was love at first sight. While she doesn’t own a Ferrari she’s always been a race car drive at heart. In High School her Jeep Wrangler was well-known for reaching speeds it was not designed for. Along with Tim she is also a member of two Corvette clubs and regularly attends high performance driving events.

John Mullen - Marketing

As our marketing and business development specialist, John has spent the majority of his time, since graduating from the University of Chicago, building analytic practices in advertising agencies and exploring the Pacific Northwest with his fiancé. John fell in love with curves when he first saw a Porsche GT2 at the age of 12. He quickly recognized a great business concept and opportunity to rediscover his inner gearhead when he first heard about Gearhead Workspace from Tim. He is looking forward to turning our concept into a reality to create a space for urban auto enthusiasts.

Mike Noll - Operations/Head Mechanic

Mike grew up working on cars and trucks with his father, who owned and operated a small trucking company in southern New Hampshire. He’s worked as a professional mechanic on motorcycles, snowmobiles, cars, and big-rig trucks. Along the way, he managed to pick up a law degree from Depaul, but you know what they say, you can take the boy out of the garage…

Mike lives in Chicago with his wonderful wife Brianna, their three pet rats, and a fleet of decades-old German sports-sedans that he wanted in high school. He’s been known to own a motorcycle now and then, and is undefeated at fighting Chicago parking tickets. When he’s not wrenching on cars you can find him in the kitchen, cooking up a good meal for good friends and family, or nose-deep in a book of some sort.
